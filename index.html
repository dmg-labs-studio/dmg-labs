BORED ENGINEER & v2.0

Hardware —
but fun.

Retro-futuristic designs, handcrafted devices - designed to be not boring.

I'm an engineer who missed a creative and technical outlet — so I bought a 3D printer and some Raspberry Pis. The result are devices that are reminiscent of a time that cherished craftsmanship, joy in color, timelessness in design and lack of enshitification. The aim is to create things that are beautiful and intriguing by themselves while striving for a clear functionality (like radios and vinyl players were once elegant pieces of furniture). Recurring tropes are nature-inspired shapes and my passion for music (although by no means an audiophile yet). Everything carries a personal story. Super happy if you share anything you see here or just reach out!
